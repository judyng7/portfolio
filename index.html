Welcome to Adopt a Cat Network!

Adopt a Cat Network is a cat adoption agency based in Ann Arbor, MI. Although we don't have a physical shelter, many of our cats our currently in foster homes ready for adoption!

We welcome you to take a look around, and please reach out if you're interested in adopting one of our fuzzy friends! We can set up a meet and greet for you to meet your future member of your family.
