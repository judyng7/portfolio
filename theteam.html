The Team

Tessa Kim

Tessa Kim is the Executive Director. She is responsible for making sure that everything runs smoothly. Tessa has been with the organization for 5 years and is originally from California. During her spare time, she loves going on long walks with her dog Frida.

Scott Knope

Scott Knope is the Communications Coordinator. He is responsible for making sure that our weekly newsletter is sent out! Scott has been with the organization for over 10 years and has loved every minute of it. His favorite activity is playing with his cat Mabel.

Sally Thompson

Sally Thompson is the Volunteer Coordinator. She is responsible for onboarding and coordinating over 100 of our volunteers. Sally has been with the organization for just a little over a year and looks forward to more purrfect days working with the organization. During her spare time, she raises her 5 ferrets, 6 rabbits, and twenty goats on her farm.

Jason Connor

Jason Connor is the Adoption Coordinator. He is responsible for coordinating meet and greets between potential adopters and our fuzzy friends. Jason has been with the organization for 3 years and has loved every minute of it. On the weekends, he loves going on long bike rides. He has 2 dogs and enjoys fostering puppies!
